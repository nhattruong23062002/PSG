Galtier

Christophe Galtier

Birthdate August 23rd 1966, Marseille (France)
Nationality France
Signed at PSG July 5th 2022
Position Coach

After making his name as a player in the French top flight, he has become one of its most respected coaches.

Born in Marseille, he first took charge of Saint-Etienne, leading them to the Coupe de la Ligue in 2012/13 before taking LOSC to the Ligue 1 title in 2020/21.

He then joined OGC Nice before the three-time Ligue 1 Coach of the Year moved to the capital after 12 months on the Côte d’Azur for a new 100% French chapter in Rouge-et-Bleu history.
